عن أنس بن مالك رضي الله عنه أن رسول اللّه ﷺ قال

مَن رَزَقَهُ اللّه اِمْرَأَةً صالِحَةً فَقَدْ أَعانَه عَلى شَطْرِ دِينِهِ، فَلْيَتَّقِ اللّهَ فِي الشَّطْرِ الباقِي (رواه الطبراني)

On the authority of Anas bin Maalik (may Allah be pleased with him) that the Messenger of Allah (may the peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) said:

When Allah grants one a righteous wife, He has helped him (by that) to preserve half of his religion. Let him then revere Allah in the other half. (Narrated by At-Tabarani)
